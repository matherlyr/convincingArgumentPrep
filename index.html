Debunking Movies

  1. Core Facts:
    • Moives are a way to display events or a story.
    • Different genres of movies include: horror, action, romance, etc.
    • Movies are a way to express emotions or thoughts on a given topic.
  2. Explicit Warning!! There are some myths about movies
  3. The Myth: Movies are less educational than books. (but let's not dwell on the myth)
  4. Alternative explination:
    • Movies allow students who are visual learners to create a better mental picture of concepts. For example, to understand the role that the Enigma played in the conflict between the British and the Nazis, a teacher could show the movie Imitation Game to a class.
    • Movies can take learning beyond the textbook by applying concepts to real-life events. For example, watching a movie like McFarland, USA gives students a real-life event that displays the struggles of life in America as an immigrant.
